The Sanctuary

Three portals into contemplative living

Descend
Palgongsan · Korea

Nestled in the mountain wilderness of Palgongsan, Alexandria Sanctuary holds three distinct paths for those who seek depth over distraction. Each portal has been shaped by centuries of contemplative tradition, adapted for the modern seeker who knows that transformation requires genuine encounter with the unknown.

These are not programs. They are passages — each demanding something real from those who enter.

Choose Your Path

Three Ways Into Silence

Gate VI

Cave Retreat

동굴 침묵 수련

Days of silence. Sensory deprivation. The ancient cave tradition stripped to its essential bones — darkness, breath, and the dissolution of the ordinary self. No schedule. No agenda. Only what remains when everything else is taken away.

Enter the Cave

Gates I & II

Compassionate Care

임종 동행 돌봄

Accompaniment through cognitive dissolution and the final passage. For those navigating the unmapping of the self — dementia, terminal illness, the approaching threshold. Sacred witnessing by those trained in the art of being present without flinching.

Gate VII

Residence

성소 공동체 거주

A home for the final chapter. Membership in a small, sacred community sustained by shared practice, tended land, and mutual witness. The long arc of belonging — not as retreat, but as the shape of a life lived in full consciousness.
