The ancient Library of Alexandria gathered the wisdom of the world. Our sanctuary gathers the wisdom needed for life's most sacred passage.

Nestled in the sacred mountains of Palgongsan, Korea, Alexandria is not merely a place of care—it is a sanctuary for the soul. Here, Eastern contemplative traditions meet Western philosophical inquiry in service of humanity's most profound question: How do we live fully, knowing we must die?

We believe that preparing for death is not morbid—it is the most life-affirming act. Through cave meditation, wisdom teachings, and compassionate community, we offer a space where the final chapter of life becomes not an ending, but a culmination.
