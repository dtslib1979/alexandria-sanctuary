Finding Us

Getting Here

Alexandria is located on the slopes of Palgongsan, approximately 20km northeast of Daegu city center.

By Air

Fly to Daegu International Airport (TAE). From there, we can arrange pickup (₩80,000) or you can take a taxi (approximately ₩50,000).

By Train

Take KTX to Dongdaegu Station (from Seoul: 1hr 40min). Bus 급행1 runs from the station to Palgongsan. We can also arrange pickup.

Common Questions

Do I need meditation experience?

For weekend retreats and forum events, no experience is necessary. Cave retreats and extended residencies do require some prior meditation practice—we'll discuss your background during the application process.

What about language?

Our programs are conducted in English and Korean. Most visiting teachers speak English. Silence is the primary language of the sanctuary—you'll find that words matter less than you expect.

Is it religious?

Alexandria is not affiliated with any religion. We draw from Buddhist, Christian, Stoic, and indigenous wisdom traditions while welcoming people of all faiths and none. The practice here is about direct experience, not belief.

What should I bring?

Comfortable, modest clothing for meditation (nothing tight or revealing). Warm layers—the mountain can be cool. Personal toiletries. Any medications. We provide bedding, towels, and meditation cushions.

Is there WiFi?

Limited WiFi is available in common areas for essential communications. We encourage—but do not require—digital detox during your stay. The mountain offers better connection than any signal.

Can I come alone?

Most people come alone. The community becomes your companions. If you're nervous about traveling solo, reach out—we can connect you with others making the journey.
